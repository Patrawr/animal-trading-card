Canada Goose

Canada geese can fly up to 70 mph and up to 1,500 miles in a single day, during migrations.

  • Scientific Name: Branta canadensis
  • Average Length: 75 - 110 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 24 years
  • Habitat: North America, great lakes

A large wild goose species native to North America, the Canada Goose is protected in Canada and as such often associated with the country.
